About

Curriculum Vitae

Ramiz Qudsi graduated from University of Delaware with a PhD. degree in Physics in 2021. He is currently working as Postdoctoral Associate at Center for Space Physics in Boston University. His research area focusses on space plasmas where he has published multiple papers on the interplay between microkinetic processes and turbulence. Check his Research page for more details on his work and his peer reviewed published papers.

When not busy sciencing, he loves to read and write. His favorite genre in literature is fantasy and J. R. R. Tolkien, Neil Gaiman and Brandon Sanderson are his absolute favorite authors. He likes writing poems and tiny tales. You can read some of them on his Blog.

Research

Ramiz is currently doing research as a Postdoctoral associate at Boston University with Dr. Brian Walsh. Main focus of his work is studying magnetic reconnections in Earth's magnetopause where he is working on refining predictions of reconnection X-lines.

He is also involved in retrograde collisional analysis in solar wind, where he studies how the VDF of proton and heavier ions change because of collision as they travel from Sun to 1 au.

Ramiz is also working on development of a sophisticated machine learning algorithm which will help in full 3-D reconstruction of interplanetary magnetic field using observation from 4 or more spacecraft in solar wind. The algorithm will work for an arbitrary number of spacecraft in a random orientation. Another machine learning project he is involved in attempts to find large-scale average trends in plasma parameters across the Heliosphere using data from 12-15 spacecraft.

Inconsequential Ephemerals

In my free time, once a year or less, I am engulfed by a desire to pen down my thoughts either in paragraphs of a poorly written prose or in random stanzas of a poem. This section here is a collection of such thoughts and feelings as I might have experienced over last two decades or so.



When the sun has set, and the sky ablaze with fiery stars
When the earth has cooled, and the zephyr blows at lazy pace

When the mortals have slept, and the darkness whispers silently
When the Endless roam Earth, and people dream his infinite realm

When the demons descend, and the angels flutter wings to fly
When the morality blurs, and the ecstasy of passionate hearts peak

Shrouded in life, and propelled by impetus of immortal love
I shall meet you, beyond the scorching gaze of the universe!



In an ever expanding universe
We move closer defying darkness
Violating Hubble's sacred law
As we gravitate towards the center

Amidst the confluence of our
Own twisted, convoluted emotions
Rupture the fabric or space and time
Conceive our own ephemeral continuum

We embrace, we annihilate the other
In a brilliant burst of infinite energy
The dimensions ignite, forces crumble

And the cosmos burns in brilliance
Of a billion ablazed supernovae
